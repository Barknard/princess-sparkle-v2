## Technical Notes

- All files: MP3 format, 44100 Hz, mono, 64 kbps
- Normalized to -12dB with loudnorm filter
- Low-pass filtered at 4000 Hz (child-safe, per WORLD-LIFE.md spec)
- High-pass filtered at 80 Hz (remove rumble)
- Max duration: 5 seconds (goodnight melody), most under 3 seconds
- All files under 100KB
