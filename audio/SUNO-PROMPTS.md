## Tips
- Set "Instrumental" toggle ON in Suno
- Each prompt is under 200 chars
- Generate 2-3 variations per prompt, pick the best
- For looping BGM: add "seamless loop" to the prompt
- For shorter jingles (quest complete): add "short, 15 seconds"
