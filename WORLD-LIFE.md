# Princess Sparkle V2 — World Life & Interactivity Bible

Everything that makes the world feel alive. Every tappable object,
every silly moment, every animal, every sound, every discovery.
This is the difference between "educational app" and "favorite game."

---

## Design Principle

> "3-5 interactive objects visible on every screen at all times."
> Every tap produces a visible + audible response.
> The world rewards curiosity, not accumulation.

### Ambient Animals (always present, always interactive)

| Animal | Behavior | Tap Interaction | Sound |
|--------|----------|----------------|-------|

| **Birds** | Sit on trees, fences, rooftops | Tap near them → fly up, circle, land somewhere else | Tweet + wing flap |
| **Cat (village)** | Sleeps in sunny spots, slowly walks around | Tap to pet → purrs, heart animation. Sleeps in the PATH sometimes (walk around it!) | Purr + "mew" |
| **Dog (village)** | Follows princess for a while, tail wagging | Tap to pet → happy bark, tail wags faster | Panting + "arf" |
| **Frogs (pond/stream)** | Sit on lily pads or rocks | Tap → croak + jump to another pad | "Ribbit" + splash |
| **Fish (pond)** | Swim lazily under water | Tap water → fish jumps, sparkles | Splash + shimmer |
| **Squirrels** | Run up and down trees, carry acorns | Tap → squirrel does a backflip and chatters | Chitter + bounce |
| **Ducks (pond)** | Swim in a line, occasionally dive | Tap → quack, head goes underwater, butt sticks up | "Quack" + splash |
| **Rabbits (forest)** | Hop between bushes, freeze when princess is near | Tap → nose wiggle, then hop closer | Soft hop + sniff |

| **Fireflies (evening)** | Appear during sunset, glow and drift | Tap → brighter glow, nearby fireflies gather | Twinkling bell |

### Baby Animals (Extra Cute)

| Baby Animal | Behavior | Special |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| **Duckling** | Follows mama duck, occasionally waddles the wrong way | Tap to nudge back to mama → mama quacks thanks |
| **Kitten** | Pounces at butterflies, falls over | Clumsy pounce animation → tumble → gets up, tries again |
| **Puppy** | Chases own tail, trips over things | Spinning animation → dizzy wobble → shake it off |
| **Baby bird** | Sits on ground, flaps tiny wings but can't fly yet | Flap flap flap... hop. Flap flap flap... hop. |

### Animal Follow Mechanic
After helping an animal (returning a lost baby to its parent, sharing food), that animal **follows the princess for 1-2 minutes**. Just trails behind her and the companion. Pure delight, no game mechanic — just friendship.

Maximum followers: 3 (princess, companion, + 1 animal friend). More would be chaotic for a 4yo to track.

## 4. MUSIC & RHYTHM MOMENTS

### The Musician NPC: Melody the Cricket

A recurring character in the village square who plays a tiny violin/fiddle.

**Interactions:**
- Tap Melody → she plays a short tune, nearby animals dance
- Quest: Help Melody find her lost music sheets (scattered around village)
- Each sheet = a new tune she can play
- She teaches the companion a "companion song" that plays during walks

### Rhythm Encounters

| Encounter | How It Works |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Frog chorus** | 3 frogs on lily pads croak a pattern. Tap each frog to make them croak along. No wrong answers — just joyful noise. |
| **Stepping stones** | Cross a stream by tapping stepping stones. Each stone plays a note. Walking across plays a melody. |
| **Wind chimes path** | A row of wind chimes in the forest. Walk past and they play a descending melody automatically. Tap one → it rings its note. |
| **Drum log** | A hollow log in the forest. Tap it → deep drum sound. Tap it repeatedly → the companion starts dancing. |
| **Singing flowers** | A row of flowers that hum when you walk past. Each flower is a different pitch. Walk back and forth = playing music. |
| **Rain percussion** | During light rain, raindrops on different surfaces make different sounds. Puddle = splash, leaf = ping, mushroom = boing. Walking around = playing drums. |

### Companion Musical Behaviors
- Companions hum softly while walking (very quiet, part of ambient sound)
- After quest completion, companion does a little dance/musical celebration
- When idle near Melody the Cricket, companion sways to the music

## 5. COMPANION CARE SYSTEM

Not a chore. Not a Tamagotchi obligation. Just bonding moments.

### Care Interactions (Optional, never nagged)

| Care Action | When It Appears | Animation | Sound |
|-------------|----------------|-----------|-------|
| **Pet** | Companion sits next to princess during rest | Stroke animation, hearts float up | Purr/happy sound specific to companion |
| **Feed** | After a long walk, companion yawns | Hand out berry/treat, companion eats happily | "Nom nom" + happy wiggle |
| **Play** | During free exploration time | Throw a sparkle ball, companion chases and brings it back | Bouncy throw + companion laugh |
| **Nap** | During sunset/wind-down | Both sit, companion curls up against princess | Soft sigh + gentle sleeping breath |
| **Groom** | After walking through muddy area | Brush animation, sparkles come off | Sparkle shimmer + companion "ahh" |

### How Care Works
- Companion occasionally shows a small emote (heart, sleepy Z, playful star)
- Player can tap the emote → care interaction plays
- Completely optional. Ignoring it has NO penalty
- Doing it strengthens the "bond" (shown as a small heart meter that fills very slowly)
- A full bond meter = the companion learns a new trick (purely cosmetic — new idle animation)

## 8. GARDEN SYSTEM

A small garden plot in the village where the princess can grow things.

### How It Works
1. Tap empty plot → choose a seed (3 options shown as pictures: flower, veggie, tree)
2. Tap to plant → planting animation + "pat pat" sound
3. Tap to water → watering can appears, pours water, sparkles
4. Wait (next session) → plant has grown one stage
5. Full bloom (2-3 sessions later) → harvestable, can gift the flower/veggie to NPCs

### Growth Stages (Visual)
1. Dirt mound
2. Tiny sprout
3. Small plant with leaves
4. Flower bud / veggie shape
5. Full bloom / ripe

### Why This Matters
- Teaches patience (growth happens across sessions)
- Gives reason to return ("I wonder if my flower grew!")
- Non-urgent (plant never dies or wilts — it just waits)
- Gifting harvest to NPCs = kindness quest opportunity

## 9. WEATHER SYSTEM

Gentle weather changes that affect the world's interactivity.

| Weather | When | Effects |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Sunny** | Default (sessions 1-3) | Butterflies active, flowers open, cats sleep in sun |
| **Light rain** | Sometimes (session 4+) | Puddles appear (tappable!), rainbow after rain, worms pop up, frogs more active. Princess gets tiny umbrella. |
| **Breezy** | Random | Leaves blow across screen, wind chimes ring, dandelion seeds drift, laundry flaps harder |
| **Firefly evening** | During sunset | Fireflies appear everywhere, tappable, magical ambient glow |
| **Light snow** | Winter season (if implemented) | Snowflakes drift, footprints in snow, animals wear tiny scarves |

Weather is NEVER threatening. No storms, no dark skies, no thunder.

## 10. SOUND DESIGN MASTER TABLE

Based on developmental psychology research (Werner 2007, Juslin & Sloboda 2010, Porges 2011).

### Core Rules
- **Never** play more than 1 SFX at a time (+ ambient music)
- **Never** use variable reward sounds (same action = same sound, always)
- **Never** use frequencies above 4000 Hz with sharp attack
- **Never** use sudden loud sounds of any kind
- All sounds normalized to -6dB to -12dB
- **Format**: MP3 (Safari/iOS compatible)
