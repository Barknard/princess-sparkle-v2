## Recording Notes for Eddie

You are recording ALL voices yourself. Here is how to approach each character:

**NARRATOR** — Your warmest, gentlest voice. Think of a loving grandparent reading a bedtime story. Female-leaning warmth. Slow pace. Leave pauses between phrases. Smile while you speak — it changes the sound.

**COMPANIONS** — Each has a distinct personality. Pitch-shift slightly in post if needed, but the key is emotion and pacing, not funny voices.

**NPCs** — Give each one a subtle flavor. Grandma Rose is your warmest. Baker Maple is your jolliest. Little Finn is small and quiet. You do not need to do "perfect" voices — she will love hearing her dad no matter what.

**GENERAL RULES:**
- Speak slowly. Then slower than that.
- Short sentences. Breathe between them.
- Smile when the character is happy. She can hear it.
- Never sound rushed, urgent, or scary.
- Record in a quiet room. These play on iPad speakers.
- Aim for 2-4 seconds per line. Max 6 seconds for longer narrator lines.
- Leave 1 second of silence at the start and end of each file.

**FILE NAMING:** Each line has a suggested filename. Record each line as a separate MP3. Save to `audio/voice/[filename].mp3`.
